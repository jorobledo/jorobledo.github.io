About me

I'm an Argentinean physicist, currently living in Bariloche . I was born in Córdoba , Argentina on June 8 1990. I have a Licenciate in physics Degree (roughly equivalent to a Ms.Sc.) and a PhD in physics. I recieved both degrees from the Faculty of Mathematics, Astronomy, Physics and Computation (FaMAF) of Córdoba's National University. I've also completed a master's study in Applied Statistics at the Faculty of Economics of the same institution.

I enjoy listening and playing Music. I've also dabbled in a few Luthiery projects of my own, having constructed a pair of classical guitars.

In my spare time, I really enjoy mountain related activities. Specially skiing, hiking, biking, and running.
