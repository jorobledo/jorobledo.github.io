Welcome, I'm Jose Robledo!

I'm a physicist who enjoys analyzing data and learning from them.

I am currently working as an artificial intelligence consultant in Stefan Kesselheim's Helmholtz AI consultant team, located at the Jülich Supercomputing Centre.

Previously, I was a post doc at the Jülich centre for Neutron Science (JCNS-2) institute of Forschungszentrum Jülich (FZJ), in Jülich, Germany. I was part of the Global Neutron Scientists (GNeuS) , a 2 year program co-financed by the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions.

Before that, I was a postdoc at the Bariloche Atomic Centre (CAB). I worked for the Neutron physics department and developed codes to process data from Deep Inelastic Neutron Scattering and Small Angle Neutron Scattering experiments.
