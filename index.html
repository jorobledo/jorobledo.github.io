Hi, I'm Jose Robledo!

I'm an argentinean physicist who enjoys analyzing data and learning from them.

I am currently working as an artificial intelligence consultant in Stefan Kesselheim's Helmholtz AI consultant team, located at the Jülich Supercomputing Centre.

Previously, I did a postdoc at the Jülich centre for Neutron Science (JCNS-2) institute of Forschungszentrum Jülich (FZJ), in Jülich, Germany. I was part of the Global Neutron Scientists (GNeuS) , a 2 year program co-financed by the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions.
